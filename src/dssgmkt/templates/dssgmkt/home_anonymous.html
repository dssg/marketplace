What is DSSG Solve?

DSSG Solve: an online marketplace where governments and non-profits can post projects, where volunteers with expertise in the field can ask questions and scope those projects into well-defined problems, and where data scientists can team up to solve those problems collaboratively

For organizations

DSSG Solve enables organizations to manage their social good projects within the platform, including volunteers, deliverables and reviews. Staff your projects with highly qualified volunteers and turn them into a team that works effectively throughout all phases of the project, from project scoping to the final QA validation.

For volunteers

DSSG Solve is a productive community of volunteers that are passionate about using technology and data for social good.
