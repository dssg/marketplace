What is DSSG Solve?

DSSG Solve: an online platform for social good organization to post projects, for volunteers to help refine and scope projects into well-defined problems, and for data scientists and technologists to help solve those problems collaboratively

For social good organizations

DSSG Solve enables organizations to use data-intensive methods to better achieve their missions. Post your project to connect with skilled volunteers who can:
  • Understand your goals and scope out technical solutions
  • Work as a team with you and with other volunteers
  • Evaluate and validate the solutions
  • Help you understand the results

For volunteers

DSSG Solve needs a global community of volunteers that are passionate about using technology and data for social good. Join us to:
  • Apply your skills for work that matters
  • Collaborate with other smart and passionate volunteers
  • Gain data science experience and build your portfolio of work
  • Showcase your skills and increase your visibility
